This Privacy Policy (hereinafter, the Policy) contains the rules governing the use of User personal information by The LightWorkers.

1.1. By using the Game, you, of your own free will and in your own best interests, consent in writing to the following types of processing your Personal Information may be subject to: collection, recording, organization, storage, specification (updates, amendments), retrieval, use, transfer (distribution, submission, access), depersonalization, blocking, archival or deletion for the purposes set forth herein, using any, all or no means of automation, as The LightWorkers, in its sole discretion, may see fit.

1.2. The terms and definitions used here and further in this Policy shall be construed in accordance with, first, the law of the UAE, second, international legal acts and, third, customary business use or scientific doctrine.

> 2. PERSONAL_INFORMATION

2.1. In this Policy, Personal Information is understood as set forth below:

2.1.1. Information about self that the User voluntarily provides when playing the Game or otherwise, including personal data;

2.1.2. User's depersonalized data transmitted automatically based on software settings.

2.2. The LightWorkers shall have the discretion to determine what kind of Personal Information Users are to provide to be able to play the Game, including but not limited to email address, information about the mobile device used (model, type and operating system version), screenshots of the Game profile, in-game purchase data (date, description/goods purchased, transaction identifier). Users, at their own discretion, may choose to also provide other information that has not been identified by The LightWorkers as mandatory.

2.3. During the first connection to The LightWorkers' Game server, each User shall be assigned a unique identifier (user_id) associated with the information in said User's profile.

2.4. Public Information. Users may, at their own discretion, provide any other additional information about themselves for the purpose of communicating with other players, such as first and last name, gender, date of birth, city they live in or any other information in the format of their own choosing. By disclosing this information in the Game chat room, the User makes it available to an unlimited number of persons, i.e. makes it public.

2.5. The LightWorkers does not verify Personal Information it receives for accuracy, nor checks if the User reporting same is in possession of any consents that may be required for processing same in accordance with this Policy. It is presumed that Users act reasonably and in good faith, keep all the information up-to-date and possess any and all consents necessary from the owners of any personal information.

2.6. Users acknowledge and consent to the use in the Game of third-party statistics and analysis software (such as GameAnalytics, Google AdSense and AdMob) that may enable such third parties to transmit and receive depersonalized information described in para 2.1.2 herein.

Such information generated by third-party software may include:

  • browser information (type, version, cookies);
  • operating system information (type, version, screen resolution);
  • request information (time, click-through source, IP address).

The composition and scope of depersonalized data collected by third-party software is determined by the owners of copyright to said software.

2.7. The LightWorkers shall not be liable for the use of any Personal Information by any third parties the User may interact with when playing the Game.

> 3. REASONS_FOR_PROCESSING_PERSONAL_INFORMATION

3.1. The LightWorkers will process (including collection and storage) only that Personal Information which is needed for providing access to the Game, maintaining its functionality, and supporting the User.

3.2. The LightWorkers may only use Personal Information for the following purposes:

3.2.1. Providing the User with access to the Game;

3.2.2. Maintaining Game functionality;

3.2.3. Identifying the User for support and service purposes;

3.2.4. Providing technical and other support for playing the Game. Users may communicate with the support service via the Internet using specific software (the Game's pages on Google Play, AppGallery, App Store, Facebook, Discord, Instagram, YouTube and other communities) and/or email;

3.2.5. Keeping in contact with the User to improve information services and Game performance. Contact with the User will be maintained as set forth in para 3.2.4 herein;

3.2.6. Conducting marketing research, statistical analysis or other studies using depersonalized data. Data collected by the systems indicated in para 2.6 herein is depersonalized and used to determine how much time Users spend playing the Game and what their interests and preferences may be.

> 4. PERSONAL_INFORMATION_SECURITY_REQUIREMENTS

4.1. The LightWorkers shall store Personal Information and protect it against unauthorized access and sharing in accordance with its internal rules and regulations and subject to the requirements of applicable law. Personal Information is processed at The LightWorkers office.

4.2. Personal Information shall be kept confidential, except for its public component, if any, as per para 2.4 herein, or where User-selected software settings allow for open sharing of information with other Game Players or any other Internet Users.

4.3. The LightWorkers may only share Personal Information with third parties under the following circumstances:

4.3.1. Where the User has given his or her consent for same, including where User-selected software settings do not limit sharing of certain information;

4.3.2. Where such sharing may be necessary for the User to be able to use Game functionality or where the Game's specific features require same;

4.3.3. Due to the Game being transferred to a third party to possess, use or own;

4.3.4. If ordered by a court or another authorized government body, as set forth in the law;

4.3.5. To protect The LightWorkers' rights and lawful interests in the event of the User violating applicable rules or abusing the Game.

4.4. Personal Information shall be processed and stored for as long as it may be needed for the purposes for which it had been collected.
