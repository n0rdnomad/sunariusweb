> WORLD_PARAMETERS

Welcome to Sunarius — a ruthless world that has survived the apocalypse. Here, the sun scorches the earth, and every drop of water counts. You are the leader of a small squad, fighting to save your people from slavery and make a name for yourself amidst the political chaos of disjointed factions. But be cautious — this world is full of threats that do not forgive mistakes.

Nobody knows what truly happened. Why did the old world end and a new one begin? How did the great cities of the old world turn into ruins? You must uncover the bitter truth about what happened and what immense danger threatens the world once again.

Sunarius is a post-apocalyptic, tactical, turn-based RPG for mobile devices where players' decisions truly matter and shape the world. The fate of Sunarius lies in your hands! Join the struggle for survival — where legends are born from ashes, and the future is written in steel!

> CORE_SYSTEMS

Turn-Based Tactical Combat

Battles take place on a hexagonal grid and require deep strategic planning.

Dynamic Strategy Mode

Establish your own faction, conquer key sectors, and gather resources to dominate the world.

Squad Classes & Perks

Form a squad of specialists with unique stats, increase levels, and choose powerful perks.

Quests & Skills

Embark on engaging plot quests with various endings and an infinite number of random encounters.

Crafting & Economy

Scavenge loot, trade with caravans, and discover unique items obtainable through crafting.

Factions & Reputation

Every action has consequences. Your reputation directly affects prices, dialogues, and attitudes.
